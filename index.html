Economic Prosperity and Employment Insights Across Malaysian States

Explore the economic landscape of Malaysia through various indicators such as GDP per capita, employment, and unemployment trends across different states from 2017 to 2020. These visualizations aim to provide insights into the regional economic disparities and growth patterns.

GDP per Capita by State in Malaysia (2017)

This map illustrates the GDP per capita across different states in Malaysia for the year 2017. It highlights areas with higher economic performance and those that may require more focused development efforts.

GDP per Capita vs. Employment (2017)

The bubble plot compares GDP per capita against employment rates in 2017. This visualization helps in understanding the relationship between economic output and employment levels across various different states.

Unemployment Rate Trends by State (1982-2020)

This line chart showcases the trends in unemployment rates across different states from 1982 to 2020. It provides a historical perspective on how employment has evolved over nearly four decades.

Conclusion

The visualizations presented offer a comprehensive overview of Malaysia's economic indicators over the years. By analyzing GDP per capita, employment, and unemployment trends, stakeholders can make informed decisions to foster balanced regional growth and address economic challenges.
